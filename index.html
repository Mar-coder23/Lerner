Lerner Research Institute

Computing Services

LRICS is comprised of 21 information technology specialists
providing server support, special project support and custom
support of the desktop in biomedical and clinical research.

The combined supported user base is greater than 2,000. Platforms supported
include Windows, Macintosh and the Unix / Linux operating systems. The server
environment, housed in the Cleveland Clinic Tier III data center, is monitored
continuously by automated systems implemented at the enterprise level and
specifically within the Lerner Research Institute read more.

Emergency Support Requests

The following are considered an emergency:

  • Cannot log into a domain or clinic email account.
  • A computer, monitor or departmental printer that is unable to power on or fully boot.
  • A computer that can not print to a departmental printer or only installed printer.
  • Failure of pre-installed applications or operating system components critical to work,
    including Microsoft Office suite and Lawson.
  • Needed recovery of a deleted folder or file.
  • Out of storage space on a network share.

Standard Support Requests

Usually used for installations of new computers, software, hardware, upgrades and computer moves.
Problems with printers, remote access, desktop configuration, application settings.
